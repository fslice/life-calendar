iPhone Lock Screen

A wallpaper that counts down for you

Pick any date — your birthday, a goal, a deadline — and get a lock screen that updates itself daily with a snake-shaped progress bar. One square per day, zero effort.

Set up auto-update

1
Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone.
2
Go to Automation → New Automation → Time of Day. Set to 6:00 AM, repeat Daily, select Run Immediately.
3
Tap New Blank Automation. Add "Get Contents of URL" and paste your wallpaper URL.
4
Add a second action: "Set Wallpaper Photo" → Lock Screen. Expand options and disable "Crop to Subject" and "Show Preview".
5
That's it. Your lock screen updates every morning with a fresh countdown.
