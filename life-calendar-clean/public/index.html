Birthday countdown

Generate a daily wallpaper for your iPhone lock screen showing a snake progress bar counting down to your birthday.

Set up your iPhone

STEP 1

Open the Shortcuts app on your iPhone.

STEP 2

Go to the Automation tab, tap New Automation, select Time of Day, set it to 6:00 AM, repeat Daily, and select Run Immediately.

STEP 3

Tap New Blank Automation. Add the action "Get Contents of URL" and paste your wallpaper URL above.

STEP 4

Add a second action: "Set Wallpaper Photo" and choose Lock Screen. Tap the arrow to expand options and disable both "Crop to Subject" and "Show Preview".

STEP 5

Done! Your lock screen will update every morning with a fresh countdown.
